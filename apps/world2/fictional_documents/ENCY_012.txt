# Lorekeeper Practices and Rituals

Lorekeeper practices encompass herbalism, celestial observation, and oral tradition preservation.
The annual convocation at the Crystalwood Grove is mandatory for all Lorekeepers.
Taraos is regarded among the most adept, particularly for elixir mastery.
Lorekeepers serve as arbiters, instructors, and healers in Valefolk society, commanding significant respect.
