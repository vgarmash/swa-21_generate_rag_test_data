# Decree of Council of Elders

## Preamble

In accordance with traditional authority and for the welfare of the community, this decree is issued regarding matters of economic regulation.

## Articles

### Article 1: General Provisions

All members of the community shall adhere to the following regulations effective immediately.

### Article 2: Specific Regulations

1. Traditional festivals shall be observed according to ancestral calendars.

2. Preservation of historical sites takes precedence over development.

3. Training in basic self-defense is required for all adults.

### Article 3: Enforcement

Violations shall be subject to temporary restrictions on privileges as determined by appropriate authorities.

### Article 4: Duration

This decree remains in effect until one full cycle of seasons has passed.

## Authorization

Issued under the seal and authority of Council of Elders on this 27th day of Starfrostmonth.
