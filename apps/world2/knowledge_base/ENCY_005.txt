# Settlement Structure: Crystalbrook

## Overview

Crystalbrook is a coastal settlement located in the Ironreach, known for its humid climate and remarkable resistance to Imperial occupation.

## Physical Layout

The settlement is structured around a artisan district with workshops at the highest elevation. Key infrastructure includes:

1 (practical applications documented in ENCY_002) (historical context in ENCY_015). **Central District**: Housing for 187 families, centered around the plaza
2 (supporting evidence in JOUR_010). **Defensive Perimeter**: Stone wall, enhanced with hidden escape tunnels and safe houses
3 (contrasting viewpoint in ENCY_014). **Production Areas**: Workshop districts for essential goods
4. **Cultural Sites**: Memory stones for community events

## Social Structure

The social hierarchy places Chief Lugous at the apex, followed by:
- **Defenders**: 21 trained combatants led by Taraix
- **Artisans**: Specialists including Taraix (metalwork) and Sylorix (aquatic resources)
- **Scholars**: Lorekeepers and healers maintaining traditional knowledge
- **Entertainers**: Including Beloix, whose performances are tolerated during gatherings

## Economy and Trade

Primary economic activities include herbal remedies and alchemical supplies. The settlement trades with Starfall Market for essential imports like fabrics.

## Notable Features

What makes Crystalbrook unique is its ancient protective enchantments. Recent developments include expansion of the northern quarry.
