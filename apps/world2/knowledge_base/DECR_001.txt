# Decree of Lorekeeper Assembly

## Preamble

In accordance with traditional authority and for the welfare of the community, this decree is issued regarding matters of cultural preservation.

## Articles

### Article 1: General Provisions

All members of the community shall adhere to the following regulations effective immediately.

### Article 2: Specific Regulations

1. Public gatherings exceeding twenty individuals require advance notification.

2. Price controls on essential goods shall be maintained.

3. Passing down of oral histories to younger generations is encouraged.

### Article 3: Enforcement

Violations shall be subject to community service requirements as determined by appropriate authorities.

### Article 4: Duration

This decree remains in effect until the next seasonal council convenes.

## Authorization

Issued under the seal and authority of Lorekeeper Assembly on this 27th day of Starfrostmonth.
