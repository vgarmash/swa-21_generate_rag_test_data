# Decree of Council of Elders

## Preamble

In accordance with traditional authority and for the welfare of the community, this decree is issued regarding matters of social conduct.

## Articles

### Article 1: General Provisions

All members of the community shall adhere to the following regulations effective immediately.

### Article 2: Specific Regulations

1. Trade with external parties requires approval from designated authorities.

2. Preservation of historical sites takes precedence over development.

3. Waste disposal shall conform to environmental protection standards.

### Article 3: Enforcement

Violations shall be subject to temporary restrictions on privileges as determined by appropriate authorities.

### Article 4: Duration

This decree remains in effect until amended or revoked by subsequent decree.

## Authorization

Issued under the seal and authority of Council of Elders on this 11th day of Bloommonth.
