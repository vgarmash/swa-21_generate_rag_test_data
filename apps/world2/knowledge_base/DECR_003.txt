# Decree of High Commander

## Preamble

In accordance with traditional authority and for the welfare of the community, this decree is issued regarding matters of cultural preservation.

## Articles

### Article 1: General Provisions

All members of the community shall adhere to the following regulations effective immediately.

### Article 2: Specific Regulations

1. All able-bodied individuals shall participate in defensive drills twice per moon cycle.

2. Use of standing stones for construction requires quarrymaster approval.

3. Community service obligations apply to all resident families.

### Article 3: Enforcement

Violations shall be subject to temporary restrictions on privileges as determined by appropriate authorities.

### Article 4: Duration

This decree remains in effect until specific conditions are met as outlined in supplementary documents.

## Authorization

Issued under the seal and authority of High Commander on this 25th day of Sunfiremonth.
