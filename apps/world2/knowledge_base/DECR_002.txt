# Decree of Council of Elders

## Preamble

In accordance with traditional authority and for the welfare of the community, this decree is issued regarding matters of cultural preservation.

## Articles

### Article 1: General Provisions

All members of the community shall adhere to the following regulations effective immediately.

### Article 2: Specific Regulations

1. The harvesting of moonleaf shall be limited to designated areas during specific lunar phases.

2. Resolution of disputes shall follow established mediation procedures.

3. Community service obligations apply to all resident families.

### Article 3: Enforcement

Violations shall be subject to corrective labor assignments as determined by appropriate authorities.

### Article 4: Duration

This decree remains in effect until the next seasonal council convenes.

## Authorization

Issued under the seal and authority of Council of Elders on this 18th day of Rainmonth.
